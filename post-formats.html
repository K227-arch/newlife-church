The power of a scripture

Posted by Jane Doe in Post formats Views 41

The Word of God is living and active, shaping hearts and transforming lives at New Life Church (Hebrews 4:12). Scripture is a lamp to our feet and a light to our path, guiding us in truth and righteousness (Psalm 119:105). Faith comes by hearing the Word, strengthening our walk with God and deepening our trust in Him (Romans 10:17). Every promise in Scripture is God-breathed, equipping us for every good work and preparing us for His purpose (2 Timothy 3:16-17). When we stand on God's Word, we build our lives on an unshakable foundation that cannot be moved (Matthew 7:24-25).
